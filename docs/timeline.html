Chhatrapati Shivaji · Volume 1 · The Boy of Shivneri

Timeline

The years of this book, from a mother's journey to a giant's challenge — 1629 to 1656.

  • 1629Jijabai travels to the safety of Shivneri fort through a war-torn Deccan. (Chapter 1)
  • Feb 1630Shivaji is born inside Shivneri fort. (Chapter 1)
  • 1636Shahaji enters Bijapur's service and is sent far south; Jijabai and Shivaji are settled at Pune. (Chapter 4)
  • late 1630sPune, wrecked by war, is rebuilt and resettled under Jijabai and Dadoji Konddev. (Chapter 5)
  • 1640sShivaji trains under Dadoji and gathers his band of Maval companions in the hills. (Chapter 7)
  • 1645The oath of Swarajya is sworn at the temple of Raireshwar. (Chapter 9)
  • 1646Shivaji takes his first fort, Torna, at the age of sixteen. (Chapter 11)
  • 1646–47Rajgad is built; Kondhana and Purandar are won by wit. (Chapter 12)
  • 1647Dadoji Konddev dies; Shivaji takes full charge and raises his own royal seal. (Chapter 14)
  • 1648Shahaji is arrested by treachery; Shivaji wins his first true battle at Purandar. (Chapter 15)
  • 1649A daring letter to the Mughal prince helps free Shahaji. (Chapter 16)
  • 1650–55The quiet years: Shivaji builds an army, forts, fair rule, and a network of spies. (Chapter 17)
  • Jan 1656Shivaji conquers the forest kingdom of Javli and first sees the great hill of Raigad. (Chapter 19)
  • 1656Pratapgad is begun; Bijapur sends the giant general Afzal Khan. (End of Volume 1.) (Chapter 20)
